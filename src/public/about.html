Anime Logger is a website created by Arden Weed, Angel Galindo, Marco Rovell, and Dillon Tran. The purpose of this website was to build our foundantional knowledge on full stack development and to get us career prepared. On this website you can search up your favorite shows and add them to your own personal list to keep track of the ones you have watched. If you want you can even rate them out of 10 to compare and share with friends.
